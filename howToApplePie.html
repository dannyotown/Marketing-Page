How To: Make The Best Apple Pie

"This was my grandmother's apple pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!"

Instructions

1) Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.

2) Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.

3) Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.

4) Let the pie cool off. It usually takes 30 minutes for this process.
